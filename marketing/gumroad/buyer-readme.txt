This extension does NOT route your data through Bluedev.

  Local & free (recommended for privacy):
    * Ollama        — https://ollama.com (set OLLAMA_ORIGINS=
                      chrome-extension://* before starting)
    * LM Studio     — https://lmstudio.ai

  Cloud:
    * Anthropic Claude — https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys
    * OpenAI ChatGPT   — https://platform.openai.com/api-keys

    * Groq             — https://console.groq.com/keys (free tier!)

local URL), choose a model, click "Bağlantıyı test et".
Once tested, every Pro AI feature is available.
